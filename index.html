Welcome.

Thesem Fashion is a place to buy designer fashion that is elegant, meaningful, sustainabe, organic. Good for the mind, body, environment, others. It is inspired by conceptual thinking, supporting women to be the best they can be, to be passionate, balanced and healthy. It is against fast fashion, which is the 2nd most pollutant after oil. It aims for high end quality, that is precious and memorable. You can buy, rent, trade, sell your buy back to us. Have a styled and trendy wardrobe sent to you by subscription service. No more stress, worry, let us handle it. Pair it up with makeup service and styling with accessories/shoes. Get the home decor with plants. Keep your wardrobe fresh and new, opt to share your knowledge and clothing with others, or buy new to exactly your liking. Change your world, change your mind, amplify your life.

SUMMER 17/18

The Concept

Qi gives us life force, to let us breathe and feel light, floating and free. No burdens, just you and the crisp air around you. A weightless and purity in thought, body and spirit. Concept - Qi, an energy life flow, whirls through the space between us. It is an energy, a spirit that dances within us. It is hauntingly beautiful, light, simple, pure. Qi is a Chinese word that translates to ‘breathe’ or ‘air’ and permeates everything we do. A breath can be soft, strong, velvety. Inspired by artworks Zhang Da Qian and Toko Shinoda as well as sheer fabrics and the cathartic expression of a deep black ink stroke in a sweeping movement. We all need Qi to live, and yet in the hustle and bustle we lose ourselves. This collection encourages you to bring it back with a little bit of relaxation and indulgence in what you truly need. It is always About The Contradictions, The Fast Pace of Life + Taking it all in... never mind what they say, express yourself for your life is the only one worth fully living. Multi-use pieces, garments that make life easy, for sleeping in during a balmy beautiful summer, throw one on after a swim, step out in it after a yoga session, wine and dine in it or wear it during a plane trip. Chuck it into your little purse. Made from breathable silk and pure cotton.
